	-- game concept--

Idea:
You empersonate a selfish middle eastern Zar in south gambia trying to build up your petty dynasty, meet your infantile needs and survive your ungrateful citizens' riots. 

Tags:
Round based, strategic, build-up, tower-defense

Gameplay:
- Each round you do one action, then your stupid-ass citizens get closer to
  your Alhambra and finally a random(?) event happens, or doesn't. The
  actions you can do each round are:
  - Choose 1 out of 5 randomly(?) presented buildings and add it to your
    majestic Empire.
  - Allow foreigners to settle in your blessed lands (who immediately
    turn into rioters obviously).
  - Pass (to increase interest).

Map:
Your residence is errected on a 5x5 grid of squares. It can be increased
later on during gameplay. Each building occupies one field. Rioters spawn
outside of vision range (5 maybe?) and move one field per round.

Mechanics:

- Building:
  - 5 buildings are randomly presented to you every round. They have
    different costs. You can only buy one. Independent from your choice
    to buy one or not to buy any, you can choose 1 building to be presented
    to you again next round.
  - If you buy a building that you are not able to add to your Castle just
    now, you can store it away and use a turn later in game to add it.
  - Gardens must be connected to Gemächer or similar rules (etc.)
  - Some buildings already have some walls around it. They must fit
    into your Palast as in the boardgame Alhambra.
  - Beyond a certain board-size you need to make space for building. Your
    citizens are allowed to break down their houses to make space for your
    highness. Depending on the citizen's status bad things can happen.

- Satisfy needs:
  - Gardens increase your ERHOLUNG
  - Gemächer increase your VERGNÜGEN
  - Pavilions increase your SOZIALE KONTAKTE
  - To stifle riots increases your STOLZ. -> Türme help you beat down riots.
  - Serails increase your crave for MACHT
  - Arkaden increase your REICHTÜMER

- Interconnectivity:
  - SOZIALE KONTAKTE increases chance of good random events.
  - Too much STOLZ becomes ARROGANZ and lowers SOZIALE KONTAKTE and
    increases riot spawn rate.
  - If STOLZ is too low, bad things (?) happen.

- Besuche:
  - Je höher SOZIALE KONTAKTE, desto wahrscheinlicher werden zufällige Besuche.
  - Bei einem Besuch kommt eine Person und nimmt Einfluss auf den Spieler.
  - Mögliche Besucher:
    - Ein zypriotischer Zar bewundert deine Gärten. +GELD für deine Gärten.
    - Ein lettischer Lord kommt in einem Ford angefahren und vergnügt sich
      in deinen Gemächern. +GELD für deine Gemächer.
    - Ein böhmischer Baron ...?
    - Ein kasachischer Kaiser schenkt dir eine Karte.
    - Ein isländischer Imperator verbessert die Reichweite deiner Türme.
    - Ein sächsischer Sultan besucht dich. Aus Wut zerstört er eines deiner Gebäude.
